Duty Free is a design cooperative that specializes in interactive and generative experiences for the web. Duty Free exists as a liminal space between the worlds of graphic design, algorithmic generation, and art experience. To us, “duty” represents a calling to re-imagine design’s purpose as a tool to uplift marginalized communities and to contribute to the betterment of society. To be “duty free” symbolizes a fundamental belief that the collection of data, knowledge and resources should not only be decentralized, but also distributed.

With this belief, we aspire to dedicate our creative passions for the joy of giving without necessarily expecting anything in return.

As a collective of designers, artists and cultural workers, we want to pose the question in our practice, what does mutual aid look like within design spaces?
